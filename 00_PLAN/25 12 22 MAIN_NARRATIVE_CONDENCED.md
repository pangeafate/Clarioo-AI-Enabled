Buyers:

Spend hundreds of hours comparing vendors manually

Validate vendor claims one by one

Operate in 6+ month sales cycles

Do not know real buyer criteria

2. Why does this problem persist?

The failure is structural, not procedural.

Most buyers are not domain experts and do this work on top of their day jobs

Marketing noise and product complexity overwhelm rational comparison

Multi-stakeholder decisions amplify bias and delay

Buyers and sellers do not trust each other’s information

Existing tools optimize individual steps (RFPs, reviews, demos). None orchestrate the full decision with evidence and memory.
